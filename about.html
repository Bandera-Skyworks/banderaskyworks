About Us

At Bandera Skyworks, we specialize in delivering precision aerial inspection and data collection services designed to enhance safety, streamline operations, and reduce costs. Serving clients throughout the Texas Hill Country and beyond, our mission is to provide actionable insights through high-resolution imaging and advanced analytics—without the risk, time, or expense of traditional inspection methods.

We utilize cutting-edge DJI Enterprise drones, including the Matrice 30T for high-precision visual and thermal inspections, and the Mavic 3 Pro. These platforms allow us to capture ultra-detailed data in even the most challenging conditions, enabling faster maintenance decisions, early fault detection, and real-time situational awareness across industries such as energy, utilities, infrastructure, construction, and agriculture.

Whether you're managing powerlines, inspecting rooftops, surveying pipelines, or monitoring crop health, Bandera Skyworks brings a new level of efficiency and clarity to your operational workflow.

Licensed and Compliant: Flying Under FAA Part 107

All Bandera Skyworks pilots are certified under FAA Part 107, the regulatory framework that governs commercial drone operations in the United States. This certification ensures we operate legally, safely, and with the highest professional standards in airspace usage, flight planning, and risk mitigation.

Operating under Part 107 means:
  • We follow strict pre-flight and post-flight procedures.
  • We maintain detailed flight and maintenance logs for compliance and audit readiness.
  • We carry appropriate liability coverage for commercial UAS operations.
  • We have the authority to fly in controlled airspace with proper FAA authorization, enabling us to complete jobs others may not be able to.

Our commitment to Part 107 compliance reflects our dedication to safety, professionalism, and trust—giving our clients peace of mind that every mission is executed with precision and accountability.
